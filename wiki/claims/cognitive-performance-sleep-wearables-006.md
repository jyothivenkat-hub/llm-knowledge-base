# Cognitive performance fluctuates due to two underlying biological processes: homeostatic sleep pressure (accumulates during wakefulness, causing performance decline) and circadian rhythm variations (sinusoidal pattern determining daily performance peaks/troughs), with typical afternoon performance peaks.

**Type:** concept
**Source:** Cognitive Performance Sleep Wearables
**Evidence:** Section 2.1 cites Borb´ely and Alexander's two-process model and notes 'we typically peak in performance in the afternoon' due to interaction of homeostatic and circadian processes
