# A 2-month longitudinal field study design combining three complementary data streams—wearable sleep tracking (full duration), smartphone typing metrics (full duration), and PVT alertness tests (2 weeks)—enables validation of sleep-cognition relationships while introducing novel passive measurement approaches.

**Type:** method
**Source:** Cognitive Performance Sleep Wearables
**Evidence:** Contributions section describes 'a longitudinal study of two months, where ubiquitous sensing technologies were used to collect sleep and typing data for the full study duration and reaction test data for two weeks'
