# Caffeine's sleep-disrupting effects persist far longer than subjective awareness: 400mg consumed 12 hours before bedtime still reduces deep sleep by 20.6 minutes despite participants reporting normal sleep quality.

**Type:** finding
**Source:** Dose and Timing Effects of Caffeine on Subsequent Sleep: A Randomized Clinical Crossover Trial
**Evidence:** The Perception Gap section: only 22% of participants identified both dose and timing correctly; participants felt fine at 8-12 hour timings despite objective measurements showing significant disruption
