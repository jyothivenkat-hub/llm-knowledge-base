# High-dose caffeine (400mg) selectively destroys deep sleep (N3/slow-wave sleep), reducing it by 29.7 minutes at 4 hours before bed and 20.6 minutes even at 12 hours before bed, while increasing light sleep proportion by 5.6-5.8%.

**Type:** finding
**Source:** Dose and Timing Effects of Caffeine on Subsequent Sleep: A Randomized Clinical Crossover Trial
**Evidence:** Deep Sleep Destruction subsection: N3 reduced by 29.7 min (4h), 15.3 min (8h), 20.6 min (12h); N1/N2 increased 5.6-5.8% across all timings
