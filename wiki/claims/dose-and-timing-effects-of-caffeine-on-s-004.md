# Caffeine causes sleep fragmentation at high doses: 400mg increases wake time after sleep onset by 26.2-29.1 minutes and awakenings per hour by 1.4 when consumed 4 hours before bed.

**Type:** finding
**Source:** Dose and Timing Effects of Caffeine on Subsequent Sleep: A Randomized Clinical Crossover Trial
**Evidence:** Sleep Fragmentation subsection: wake time after sleep onset increased by 26.2 min (4h) and 29.1 min (8h); awakenings per hour increased by 1.4 at 4-hour timing
