# The dose-response relationship is non-linear: 400mg reduces total sleep by 46.2 minutes MORE than 100mg at 4-hour timing, and reduces deep sleep by 33.8 minutes more, indicating a threshold effect rather than proportional response.

**Type:** finding
**Source:** Dose and Timing Effects of Caffeine on Subsequent Sleep: A Randomized Clinical Crossover Trial
**Evidence:** Dose Comparison section: 400mg reduced total sleep by 46.2 min MORE than 100mg at 4-hour timing; deep sleep was 33.8 min shorter with 400mg vs 100mg
