# Only 22% of study participants correctly identified both the caffeine dose consumed and its timing, indicating that caffeine-induced sleep degradation occurs without conscious awareness or perceived sleep quality changes at 8-12 hour intervals.

**Type:** finding
**Source:** Dose and Timing Effects of Caffeine on Subsequent Sleep: A Randomized Clinical Crossover Trial
**Evidence:** The Perception Gap section: 'Only 44% identified dose, only 36% identified timing, only 22% identified both correctly'; 'participants felt fine despite objective measurements showing significant disruption'
