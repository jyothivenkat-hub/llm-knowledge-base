# Clinical recommendation: 100mg caffeine is safe up to 4 hours before bedtime with no measurable sleep disruption, while 400mg should be avoided within 12 hours of bedtime due to persistent sleep architecture degradation.

**Type:** finding
**Source:** Dose and Timing Effects of Caffeine on Subsequent Sleep: A Randomized Clinical Crossover Trial
**Evidence:** Clinical Recommendations section: '100mg caffeine: Safe up to 4 hours before bedtime... 400mg caffeine: Avoid within 12 hours of bedtime to prevent sleep disruption'
