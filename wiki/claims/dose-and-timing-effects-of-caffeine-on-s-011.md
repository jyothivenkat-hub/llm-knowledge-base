# The study is limited to young healthy males aged 18-40, single-dose conditions without cumulative daily intake assessment, and in-home settings without full laboratory controls, limiting generalizability to women, older adults, and chronic caffeine users.

**Type:** finding
**Source:** Dose and Timing Effects of Caffeine on Subsequent Sleep: A Randomized Clinical Crossover Trial
**Evidence:** Limitations section: 'Only tested young healthy males (ages 18-40)... Single dose per condition (not cumulative daily intake)... In-home setting without laboratory controls'
