# 400mg caffeine (four cups of coffee) reduces sleep efficiency by 7.1% more than 100mg at 4-hour timing, demonstrating that high-dose caffeine substantially degrades overall sleep quality metrics.

**Type:** finding
**Source:** Dose and Timing Effects of Caffeine on Subsequent Sleep: A Randomized Clinical Crossover Trial
**Evidence:** Dose Comparison section: 'Sleep efficiency decreased 7.1% more with 400mg at 4 hours'
