# Neural activity in the human brain aligns linearly with internal contextual embeddings from large language models during natural language processing, suggesting shared representational principles between biological and artificial systems.

**Type:** finding
**Source:** Google Brain Llm Language Processing
**Evidence:** Key discovery: neural activity aligns linearly with internal contextual embeddings of speech and language within LLMs during everyday conversations; measured via intracranial electrode recordings.
