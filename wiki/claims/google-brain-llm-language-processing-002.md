# During speech listening, the brain processes information in a two-stage sequence: speech embeddings activate the superior temporal gyrus first, followed by language embeddings activating Broca's area, revealing a hierarchical temporal progression.

**Type:** finding
**Source:** Google Brain Llm Language Processing
**Evidence:** Speech Processing Sequence section: during listening, speech embeddings predict activity in superior temporal gyrus first, then language embeddings activate Broca's area.
