# Speech and language processing follows distinct temporal sequences in the brain: during listening, speech embeddings activate superior temporal gyrus first (~auditory processing), then language embeddings activate Broca's area (~semantic/linguistic processing); during speaking, this reverses with language areas activating ~500ms before articulation.

**Source:** Google Brain Llm Language Processing
**Evidence:** Speech Processing Sequence section with specific 500ms timing for language-to-articulation delay during speaking
