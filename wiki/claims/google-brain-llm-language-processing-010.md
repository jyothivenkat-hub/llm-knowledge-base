# The temporal dynamics of language processing differ between comprehension and production: comprehension follows a bottom-up sequence (acoustic→semantic), while production follows a top-down sequence (semantic→motor), yet both align with LLM representations.

**Type:** finding
**Source:** Google Brain Llm Language Processing
**Evidence:** Speech Processing Sequence section contrasts listening (speech→language) with speaking (language→motor) sequences, showing reversal between comprehension and production.
