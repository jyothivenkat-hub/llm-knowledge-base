# Dysbiosis impairs sleep quality by promoting IL-1β and TNF-α mediated inflammation, increasing intestinal permeability, activating the HPA axis, and disrupting cortisol and melatonin rhythms.

**Type:** finding
**Source:** Gut Microbiota Influences Sleep
**Evidence:** Abstract explicitly states dysbiosis 'promotes IL-1β and TNF-α mediated inflammation, alters intestinal permeability, activates the HPA axis, and disrupts cortisol and melatonin rhythms, which together impair sleep quality.'
