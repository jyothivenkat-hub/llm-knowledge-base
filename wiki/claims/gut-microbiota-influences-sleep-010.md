# Existing reviews on gut microbiota and sleep address these interactions in isolation (circadian rhythms, immune activation, or metabolic pathways), but lack integrated synthesis of how gut microbiota, immune function, endocrine signaling, and microbial metabolites converge to influence sleep health.

**Type:** concept
**Source:** Gut Microbiota Influences Sleep
**Evidence:** Introduction identifies the gap: 'existing reviews often address these interactions in isolation...However, no current synthesis integrates the combined roles of the gut microbiota, immune function, endocrine signaling, and microbial metabolites in shaping sleep health.'
