# Adolescents are more vulnerable to circadian disruption from evening blue light than adults because their circadian systems are still developing, making this population particularly at-risk.

**Type:** finding
**Source:** Impacts of Blue Light Exposure From Electronic Devices on Circadian Rhythm and Sleep Disruption in Adolescent and Young Adult Students
**Evidence:** Practical Implications section: 'The effect is worse for adolescents whose circadian systems are still developing'
