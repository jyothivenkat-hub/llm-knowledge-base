# Intermediate layers of LLMs correlate more strongly with brain activity than final layers, particularly in language-selective regions like the inferior frontal gyrus and posterior temporal cortex, suggesting final layers over-optimize for task performance at the expense of brain-aligned representations.

**Source:** Llm Brain Fmri Alignment
