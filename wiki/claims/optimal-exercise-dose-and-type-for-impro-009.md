# Intervention duration shows an inverted-U relationship with sleep improvement, peaking at 9-10 weeks (SMD = -1.40) rather than continuing to improve with longer programs, suggesting potential adaptation or compliance effects.

**Type:** hypothesis
**Source:** Optimal Exercise Dose and Type for Improving Sleep Quality: A Systematic Review and Network Meta-Analysis of RCTs
**Evidence:** Optimal Intervention Period: 9-10 weeks most effective; 12-16 weeks (SMD = -0.55) and 24+ weeks (SMD = -0.71) show diminished effects
