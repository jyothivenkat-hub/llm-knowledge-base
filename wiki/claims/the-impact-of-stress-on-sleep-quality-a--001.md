# Stress affects sleep quality through at least five distinct mediation pathways, not just direct causation. The most damaging is the full chain: stress → rumination → social anxiety → smartphone dependence → poor sleep, where each factor amplifies the previous one.

**Type:** finding
**Source:** The Impact of Stress on Sleep Quality: A Mediation Analysis Based on Longitudinal Data
**Evidence:** Abstract and Pathways 1-5 section; structural equation modeling identified multiple indirect effects across 426 university students tracked over 6 months (October 2023 - April 2024)
